Sistem solar
============

Timeline
--------

Last edit date is 31 Aug 1999.

Description
-----------

This is supposed to be a visual simulator for the movement of the planets around
the Sun. It is, of course, complete bollocks from a scientific standpoint, but
I guess it might have some glimmer of educational value.
